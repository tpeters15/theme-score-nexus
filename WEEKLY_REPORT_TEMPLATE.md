## üìù Notes for Report Writers

### Tips for Effective Weekly Reports

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Lead with impact, not activity
- ‚úÖ Use specific numbers and metrics
- ‚úÖ Highlight problems early (don't hide bad news)
- ‚úÖ Connect work to business outcomes
- ‚úÖ Include actionable next steps

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå List every task completed (focus on meaningful outcomes)
- ‚ùå Use jargon without explanation
- ‚ùå Bury important information in long paragraphs
- ‚ùå Wait until Friday to start writing (log daily)

### How to Fill This Template

1. **Start Monday:** Review metrics and last week's report
2. **Log daily:** Keep running notes of accomplishments and signals
3. **Thursday:** Draft major sections (achievements, signals, themes)
4. **Friday morning:** Finalize metrics, blockers, and next week's priorities
5. **Friday afternoon:** Review, polish, and send

### Time Required
- **Daily logging:** 5 minutes/day
- **Friday drafting:** 30-45 minutes
- **Total:** ~1 hour/week
